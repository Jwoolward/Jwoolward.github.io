Excelsior is a program that gives an unique oppourtunities for high school students. They stay at high school for 4-6 years and have mentors assigned to them to assist them in their work. Excelsior students also have the oppourtunity to get an associate's degree. In their third year of coding, students have the chance to work as a paid intern for IBM. This program puts me at a serious advantage over those who don't have access to this oppourtunity
